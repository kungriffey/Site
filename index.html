features

Evaluate Athletes

Rapidly evaluate athletes during training and match sessions. Evaluate real-time during match sessions using a simple grading scale.

Manage and Review

Coaches access the dashboard to review evaluations. Compare player development across your team and/or organization.

Communicate

Players and parents log in to their individual profile to view evaluations and communicate with the coach. Parents now receive a friendly report card on their kids development.

Integrate

Save time by quickly importing player profiles from existing services like teamsnap or by using our simple data import features.
